Leave Mac App Store

After arguing with Apple for three months, I decide to stop updating MPlayerX in Mac App Store.


As some of you may know, from 1st, June, every application in Mac App Store have to adopt Sandboxing for releasing a new version. Sandboxing, although it is said to be the last gate keeper from the malware, brings too many troubles to the developers.


MPlayerX will lose many features if it adopted Sandboxing, it could not load the subtitle automatically, it could not play the next episode for you automatically, it could not save the snapshots to the place where you want to, etc. Without those features, MPlayerX were just another Quicktime X, which I could not accept.


I have made 6 builds trying to make MPlayerX pass the review process from Apple, but failed. There are so many useful feature waiting to be implemented, and I think I shouldn't waste more time on this.


MPlayerX will add another auto-updating mechanism from 1.0.16, and it will be released shortly.
