All in one. One for all.

Just drop anything to it, MPlayerX will give you a clear and smooth playback.

Dance on your fingers.

Pinch, tap or swipe, MPlayerX provides you the easiest way to control the playback.
Don't have to remember the keyboard shortcuts or move the cursor among the buttons, MPlayerX will follow your gestures.

Any subtitle you watch.

MPlayerX will detect and convert the encoding method of the subtitle file automatically and seamlessly.
Not only Latins, but also Eastern Asian languages, Arabic, Hebrew are well handled too.
